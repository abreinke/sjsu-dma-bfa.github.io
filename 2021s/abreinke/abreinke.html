Artist Statement

I am a digital artist and photographer whose work integrates visual elements from my personal reflections and passions. Additionally, my works are inspired by elements- such as objects, patterns, and artistic visuals- which I see at any given moment. I constantly look for ways to encapsulate these elements’ beauty and create visuals which give the viewer a feeling of joy, or solace from the world’s difficulties. At the same time, though, I hope my work can guide their reflective process- so they are inspired to change things that are within their control.

Artist Bio

I am a digital media artist who resides and practices in the Sacramento area. Most of my inspiration is drawn from past reflections, current events, and various moments that fascinate me. Additionally, most of my work explores themes from the past, yet embraces the use of current, developing technologies- such as digital software and coding languages. My main practices are digital collage and digital photography, but I also have a passion for learning new, digital mediums- such as 3D printing, virtual reality, augmented reality, and digital video- which can potentially expand my practice.
